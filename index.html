About Me

My original career in sports medicine began with a degree in Athletic Training and a Master's in Rehabilitation Science. After spending about 10 years as a sports medicine provider at a large university, I decided that I needed a little change in my life. I started volunteering my time on some graphic design projects and website design. I quickly realized that everyone needed website development or design help and that I really enjoyed it! So I began a more formal education in front end development, digital marketing and user experience.

A diverse background in healthcare and education, coupled with my communication and problem solving skills make me a unique individual in the tech world. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you and help bring your passions to life!
